Our History

Born from a passion for traditional Filipino flavors, Karinderya Express was created to bridge the gap between busy modern lives and the comforting taste of home. What started as a small family recipe book has evolved into a digital-first kitchen, dedicated to serving slow-cooked Adobo and crispy Shanghai with the speed of today's technology. We believe that everyone deserves a "lutong bahay" meal, no matter how fast the world is moving. Our mission is to deliver the warmth and hospitality of a classic karinderya in every hot, steaming bowl.
